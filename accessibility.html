Accessibility

Accessibility statement

Everyone deserves a seat at the table — including online. This site is built to meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA, consistent with the Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act (AODA).

What we’ve done

  • Colour contrast meeting AA for text and interface elements.
  • Full keyboard navigation with a visible focus indicator and a “skip to content” link.
  • Semantic headings and landmarks for screen-reader users.
  • Labels on every form field, with clear error messages.
  • Reduced-motion support for visitors who prefer less animation.
  • Text that reflows and remains readable on phones and when zoomed.

Need this in another format?

If any part of this site is hard to use, or you’d like campaign materials in large print, by phone, or in another language, please contact us — we’ll make it right.
