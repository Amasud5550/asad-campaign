Accessibility

Accessibility statement

A campaign about including everyone starts with a website everyone can use.

Our commitment

The Campaign of Asad Mahmood is committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability. We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, consistent with the goals of Ontario’s Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act (AODA).

What we've built in

This site supports keyboard navigation with visible focus indicators, includes a skip-to-content link on every page, uses labelled form fields, maintains contrast-checked colours, respects reduced-motion preferences, and is responsive down to mobile screen sizes.

Alternative formats

If you need campaign information in an alternative format — large print, plain text, or over the phone — contact us and we will provide it.

Feedback

If you encounter an accessibility barrier anywhere on this site, please tell us. Email contact@asadmahmood.ca or call (905) 347-2633, and we will work to fix it promptly.
