Before you give — Ontario's rules under the Municipal Elections Act:
  • You may give a maximum of $1,200 to this candidate, and no more than $5,000 total to all candidates running for Thorold council.
  • Only individuals who normally live in Ontario may contribute, using their own funds.
  • Corporations and trade unions cannot contribute.
  • Contributions are not tax-deductible and do not qualify for a charitable or political tax receipt.
  • The campaign must keep contribution records, including name and address information where required.
