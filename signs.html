Lawn Signs

Show Thorold you're with Asad

A lawn sign is the oldest campaign tool there is — because it works. Every sign tells your neighbours that someone they know and trust is supporting Asad.

Request one below and a campaign volunteer will deliver and set it up for free, anywhere in Thorold.

Signs are placed in accordance with the City of Thorold's sign by-law, so delivery may begin closer to the election period. We'll only use your address to deliver your sign — see our privacy statement.

Signs are free. Want to do more? Join the delivery team.
